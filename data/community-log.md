# Community observation log

Append-only. One block per observation — what you posted (or saw), where, and what actually
happened. This is the only data source for community strategy until these spaces have APIs
(they won't), so a 30-second note after each post is what makes `/strategy` smart here.

Format:

```
## YYYY-MM-DD — <community>
- posted: <what you posted, or "observation only">
- pillar: human-ai | claude-code | civic-tech | other
- response: <replies/upvotes/DMs/follows/silence — be honest>
- note: <anything about why it landed or didn't>
```
