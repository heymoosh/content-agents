I made a pledge to never write about society and leave you with a sense of despair. I will always give you something you can do about it.

Start with yourself. Ask which of the things you believe you actually believe, and which are simply an inheritance from the culture and environment you were brought up in. What is actually true, and what is just a rule you learned a long time ago and never asked yourself why?

Have discussions with your friends, neighbors, and family, and stay curious.

And if you want to take even more action: consider voting in the midterm elections in November. All of the House seats are up for election, and about a third of the Senate. It takes an hour to consider who best represents your interests, and to go vote.
