# Leader-introduction candidates (parked)

Two drafts of the **same beat**: introducing Eli, the leader, through the first data-center
strike that actually works. Both are preserved here at Muxin's direction. Neither is Chapter 1
anymore.

Why parked: Chapter 1 was repositioned. The opening chapter should set the whole world and the
core thematic conflict in real time — the displacement AI drives through jobs, democratic
process, and labor — following a person who lives through it and, at the chapter's end, gets
**recruited into the guerrilla faction**. That recruit is the POV we follow through the story;
they are NOT the leader. Eli's introduction (these drafts) moves to a later chapter.

## The two drafts

- **`draft-hallway.md`** ("The Hallway", ~1,471 words) — the later, tighter draft. Opens on the
  offstage celebration and Eli's stillness apart from it; the strike already landed.
- **`draft-infrastructure-stress.md`** ("Unexplained Infrastructure Stress", ~1,676 words) — the
  earlier draft. Opens on the thermal-loop screen and the "forty-one minutes of regional flicker"
  news framing.

Pick or merge when we build the leader-introduction chapter. Both are reference, neither is a
baseline to defend.
